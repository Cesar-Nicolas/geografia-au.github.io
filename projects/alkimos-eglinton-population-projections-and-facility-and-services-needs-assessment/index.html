Alkimos Eglinton Population Projections and Facility and Services Needs Assessment

We ran population forecasts for the 2,100ha Alkimos Eglinton subdivision using a cellular automata method to populate the land through small contiguous cells. Then we used these figures to prepare a community infrastructure plan with staged rollout of schools and other community facilities over the development cycle of the new suburb.
